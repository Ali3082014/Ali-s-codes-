  • Cucumber strips
  • Avocado slices
  • Cooked fish or crab sticks

Method:

  • Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat.
  • Spread sushi rice evenly on the sheet.
  • Add cucumber, avocado, and fish.
  • Roll the sushi tightly.
  • Cut into pieces and serve.
